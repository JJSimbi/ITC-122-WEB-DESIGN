Written Report by Simbillo, Justine Jericho M.


VANILLA ICE CREAM
This respective frozen dessert is standard across globalization, where it is served in many variations and topping designs to the people.
It contains multiple nutrient values such as calcium and protein, and the most common component required in the majority of desserts is the "sugar content."
Hence, this sweetened treat enhances the user experience of satisfactory delights, contributing to the well-being of the people.



CHOCOLATE ICE CREAM
This flavored-complex frozen dessert is esteemed by its prevalence, especially among younger individuals, due to its flavor profile.
From the dessert's internal elements — it supplies sugar and cream content, with a rich cocoa taste as a pivotal ingredient for this dessert.
As a result, this rich-flavored frozen dessert intimates numerous taste buds.



STRAWBERRY ICE CREAM
This distinguishable frozen dessert is sterilized in its natural ingredients to the degree that it is made of fresh ingredients and produced.
Amidst its displayed production, it is more towards the healthier side and more nutritive since it holds dairy, eggs, and sugar, and essentially, the fruit that blends and relishes to its betterment.
Thus, it is recommended to salvage this selected frozen dessert for nutrient demand as it possesses numerous flourishing components that are reasonable for health conditions and management.
