Life is Strange 2

A choice-driven adventure game
by Dontnod Entertainment


Chapters: 5 Episodes

Main Characters: Sean Diaz and Daniel Diaz


When a tragic incident forces two brothers to leave their home, they embark on a journey through the American West. Playing as the older brother, Sean Diaz, the player must guide and protect his younger sibling, Daniel Diaz, who is developing mysterious powers. Every decision made comprises their family bond, survival, and morality. Encounter America's most significant problems as every road passed, the level of danger increased, and so did the deep trust between brothers. Profound your bond as all roads must need a destination. What lies ahead is the decisions one must face as this is not your typical road trip but a story of brotherhood, identity, and sacrifice for the price of freedom. Are you ready to venture across all pathways to find your new home of peace?
